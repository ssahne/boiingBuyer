Seller Guides

First and foremost, we at BOiiNG encourage all our sellers to have FUN.

BOiiNG is a user-friendly, easy-to-use and enjoyable system that connects all community members to resale, reuse, recycle, or donate proceeds of the fashion items you no longer use or need.

Unlike most marketplace systems, BOiiNG will remove all the “pain” of your logistics by handling all your parcels sent and received from A – Z.

Likewise, unlike most marketplace systems, BOiiNG AI will enhance all your uploaded photos. This will not only protect the integrity of your items but will give you a much better chance to sell your items

TRANSFORMING ORDINARY PEOPLE INTO PROFESSIONAL RETAILERS

  • Step 1 – treat your customers like you want to be treated yourself
  • Step 2 – presentation and care are the keys to your success
  • Step 3 – be punctual with your deliveries
  • Step 4 – be kind and respectful

If you follow these four simple steps, you will be on your way to becoming very successful.

It will help your ratings score to ensure more buyers will want to shop with you.

Step 1: BOiiNG will email you a notification once a buyer has purchased one of the items you have promoted on the system.

Step 2: Log into your seller portal and process the shipment to the buyer.

  1. Generate the postal barcode
  2. Print a thank you letter and feel free to add your personal thank you note
  3. Print the buyer order conformation

Step 3: You don’t have a second chance to make a first impression. You should wrap the sent item with care and elegance. This will make the experience for the recipient so much more enjoyable and appreciated. Here are a few examples of nice wrapping ideas.

Step 4: Be punctual with your deliveries and send your sold item ASAP. The faster the recipient receives the item, the happier the recipient will be and the prouder you will feel.

Step 5: When the buyer receives the parcel, you will receive a notification email from BOiiNG. However, you can “track and trace” the parcel status from the seller portal at any stage.

Be kind and respectful to your customers. Be punctual, polite and gracious. It will take you a long way towards your internal happiness and satisfaction.
